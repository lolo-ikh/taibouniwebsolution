RESPONSIVE DESIGN

We develop responsive dynamic and static websites and web apps, the design of which aims, through different principles and techniques, to provide the same comfortable consulting experience even for different media. The user can thus consult the same website or web app through a wide range of devices (computer monitors, smartphones, tablets, TV, etc.) with the same visual comfort and without having to use horizontal scrolling or zooming in/out on touch devices in particular, manipulations that can sometimes degrade the user's experience, both in reading and in navigation.
